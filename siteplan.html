Tech Solutions Hub

Description: The name represents the central online resource for the technology solutions, news and tutorials also will cater to both tech enthusiasts and professionals. It is designed to be a comprehensive resource to all.

Optional domain availability: techsolutionshub.com (available)

Site Purpose

The most vital purpose of the Tech Solutions Hub is to provide a friendly and informative online platform for:

  • Offering tutorials, guides, and how-to articles on various tech topics.
  • Creating a community forum where users can share knowledge and interact.
  • Sharing the latest news and trends in technology.
  • Providing reviews of tech products and services.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: "I am a beginner in web development, Where can I find tutorials to get started with HTML and CSS?"

  • Scenario 2: "I am looking to buy a new laptop use. Are there any recent reviews of the latest models and what are the best options in my budget?"

Color Schema

The color schema will to enhance the website's visual appeal and user experience.

  • Primary Color: #2196F3 (Blue) - Used for headings, primary buttons, and interactive elements.
  • Secondary Color: #4CAF50 (Green) - Used for call-to-action buttons, and highlights.
  • Background Color: #f0f0f0 (Light Gray) - Used as the overall background.
  • Accent Color: #FFC107 (Yellow) - Used for highlighting key information and alerts.

Typography

The following fonts will be used to ensure readability and visual consistency across the website.

  • Headings (h1, h2, h3): 'Segoe UI', sans-serif (Clean and modern look).
  • Body Text (paragraphs): 'Arial', sans-serif (Simple and easy-to-read).
