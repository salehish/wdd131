ABOUT US
Founding year: 2016
Number of enrolled students: 200
Lacations: Kampala, Jinja, Gulu
Colleges offered: BYUI, Ensign
Number of Founders: 3
Email: Anavah71@gmail.com
Address: Plot 46, Main street, Jinja

Anavah is a NOT-FOR-PROFIT organization that aims to nurture and promote self reliance amongst young people in Uganda and other parts of the world. We believe that self reliance can only be achieved through the education of the hand, mind and heart. This we help students achieve through sponsoring their education at Brigham Young University, Idaho and Ensign college. We simply find young people hungry for education and offer any assistance they may require to achieve their goals.
