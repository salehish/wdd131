ABOUT US
Founding year: 2017
Number of enrolled students: 280
Lacations: Kampala, Entebbe, Jinja, Gulu,
Colleges offered: BYUI, Ensign
Number of Founders: 4
Email: salehish@gmail.com
Address: Plot 65, Main street, Jinja

Salehish is a NOT FOR PROFIT organization that aims to nurture and also promote self reliance amongst young people in Uganda and round the globe. We believe that self reliance can only be achieved through the education of the hand, mind and heart. This will help students achieve through sponsoring their education at Brigham Young University, Idaho and also Ensign college. We simply find young people hungry for education and offer any assistance they may require to achieve their goals.
